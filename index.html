Welcome to the world of Innovation and ambition! EDC-TIET is proud to present 'Strategy Sprint', a case study competition where the brightest minds across various E-Cells converge to tackle real-life problem statements. The problem statements will be revealed a day before the event. The participants will have to present their solution in a swift 5-minute pitch. The top teams will face a Q&A round judged by industry experts.

So, join us to foster your entrepreneurial spirit, connect with like-minded visionaries, and let the ultimate showdown begin. For further updates follow us on our social media handles with links given below.

General Rules

  • Team size should consist of 2-4 members.
  • Each team should have a team leader.
  • A participant cannot be a member of more than one team. In such a case both the teams will be disqualified. Cross-college teams are not allowed.
  • Teams should respect the deadlines given to them. No request regarding deadline extension will be entertained.
  • The decision taken by judges and the organizing team will be final and binding to all the participants.

About  EDC

Entrepreneurship Development Cell, Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala has been established with the aim to foster and nurture talented young minds with entrepreneurial mindsets. Witnessing an increase in the Indian start-up culture, we encourage and guide everyone bitten by the insect of entrepreneurship.We are a vibrant team dedicated to making resources easily available to students. Together, we transform dreams into reality and startups into thriving businesses.
